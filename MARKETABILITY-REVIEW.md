# Deen Plumbing — Marketability Review
*Research-backed sweep of the brand + deenplumbing.co.uk. Review only, no changes made.*

## Headline verdict
**Marketable — yes, and the site is already in the top tier for a new sole-trader trade site.** The brand is strong and the on-page conversion/trust foundations are mostly in place. The make-or-break gaps are **off-site**: a Google Business Profile and real reviews. Those two will drive more calls than anything else, because for "plumber near me" searches Google's local map pack — powered by GBP + reviews — sits above the normal results. Get those going and this is genuinely competitive.

---

## 1. Is "Deen Plumbing" marketable?
**Yes.** Scores well on the things that matter:
- **Memorable & trustworthy:** short, first-name branding ("Deen") reads as a real, local, accountable person — exactly what wins trade work. Easy to say down the phone.
- **SEO-friendly:** the word "Plumbing" is the umbrella term customers actually search, so the name itself carries keyword weight; the **.co.uk** signals UK/local.
- **Matches the domain** (deenplumbing.co.uk) — clean and consistent.

**Watch-outs (minor):**
- **"Deen" vs "Dean":** people may mishear/mis-type it as "Dean" on word-of-mouth or voice search. Not a blocker — just be ready to spell it, and consider grabbing the `dean...` typo domain later if cheap.
- **"Plumbing" only**, though you also do gas/heating/drainage — handled well already by the strap-line and services, so fine.
- **Clash check:** a quick search surfaced **no prominent UK firm** trading as "Deen Plumbing," so the name looks clear and ownable. *Action: do a 2-minute confirm on Google Maps for Milton Keynes & North London + a Companies House search before printing van livery.*
- **Gas Safe mismatch:** your reg (973458) shows on the register as **Gasbros Ltd**, not Deen Plumbing. A wary customer who checks will see a different name. *Action: add "Deen Plumbing" as a trading name with Gas Safe, or add a line on the site explaining it.*

## 2. What actually makes a UK plumbing/gas business convert + rank (proven signals)
From current local-SEO/conversion best practice:
1. **Google Business Profile (GBP)** — the single biggest local lever. Fully completed profile (services, service-area towns, hours, photos) is the fastest way to appear in the map pack. Profiles updated with photos/posts see materially more clicks and direction requests.
2. **Reviews** — ~79% of consumers are influenced by reviews; volume + recency of Google reviews is one of the strongest local ranking signals *and* the top trust driver. Ask every happy customer; reply to all.
3. **NAP consistency** — identical Name, (area), Phone across the site, GBP and any directories.
4. **Above-the-fold call action** — one-tap phone/WhatsApp visible instantly on mobile (most traffic is mobile, mid-emergency).
5. **Gas Safe verification** — number shown + invitation to check the register; official Gas Safe logo carries more weight than plain text.
6. **Service-area / town targeting** — pages or content naming the specific towns you cover help "plumber in [town]" searches.
7. **Response/availability** — "same day", "24/7 emergency" messaging reduces hesitation.
8. **Quote/pricing clarity** — free quotes, no call-out surprises; even rough guidance lowers friction.
9. **Local SEO basics** — Search Console + GA, sitemap, fast mobile site, FAQ/schema for "People also ask".
10. **Before/after & real photos** — strongest visual proof (noted as a later to-do).

## 3. Audit: current site vs the checklist

### Strong already ✅
- **Phone + WhatsApp CTAs above the fold**, persistent top bar + sticky mobile bar, pulsing call button. Excellent.
- **Gas Safe reg number** prominent + "check the register" line in FAQ.
- **Service-area targeting**: Milton Keynes & North London in title, meta, H1, footer and schema.
- **24/7 emergency + opening hours** present.
- **Clear services**: gas/boilers, plumbing, heating, drainage.
- **FAQs** (good for trust + "People also ask").
- **4 SEO articles** targeting real searches, with **FAQ + LocalBusiness JSON-LD** structured data.
- **Technical SEO**: sitemap.xml, robots.txt, canonical, Open Graph, favicon, fast static site, responsive, honest non-pushy tone.

### Prioritised gaps (ranked by impact on getting calls)
1. **🔴 Google Business Profile — not set up.** Highest impact by far. Create it, set service-area to your towns, add hours/services, verify. Without it you're invisible in the map pack.
2. **🔴 Real reviews.** Current testimonials are clearly-labelled examples. Funnel first customers to leave **Google reviews** (a short link in your WhatsApp away-message works well). Drives both trust and ranking.
3. **🟠 Search Console + Google Analytics** — not present. Add both so you can see what's working and submit the sitemap. Quick, free, essential for measuring.
4. **🟠 Town-level reach** — one combined MK/North-London page. Adding short location-specific sections/pages (key towns/boroughs you cover) helps "[town] plumber" searches. Medium effort, good SEO upside.
5. **🟠 Gas Safe trust** — use the **official Gas Safe Register logo** (you're licensed to) instead of the text badge; and resolve the Gasbros-Ltd name mismatch (see §1).
6. **🟡 Pricing/quote clarity** — add a short "What it costs / no surprise call-out fees / free quotes" line to pre-empt the #1 hesitation.
7. **🟡 Review-platform presence** — once you've a few reviews, add Checkatrade/Google badges back (they were removed while empty).
8. **🟡 Email forwarding** (deen@deenplumbing.co.uk → Gmail) — not finished; low urgency but worth it for council/admin.
9. **⚪️ Real photos / selfies of Deen + Lee** — known to-do; will lift trust a lot when added. Not dwelling on it here.
