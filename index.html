Help & advice

Frequently asked questions

Are you Gas Safe registered?

Yes — Deen Plumbing is on the Gas Safe Register (Reg No. 973458). I carry my Gas Safe ID card on every job, and you can check the registration any time at gassaferegister.co.uk.

What areas do you cover?

Milton Keynes and the surrounding towns. Not sure if you're in range? Give me a call and I'll let you know.

Do you charge for quotes?

No — quotes are free and there's no obligation. I'll explain what needs doing and what it'll cost before any work starts.

Do you offer emergency call-outs?

Yes. For genuine emergencies like burst pipes or no heating, it's always worth a call — I'll get to you as quickly as I can, 24/7.

How soon can you come out?

Often same day or next day for local jobs, depending on the diary. For urgent issues, ring me and I'll do my best to fit you in.

Is your work guaranteed?

Yes — all work is guaranteed and I'm fully insured. If something isn't right, I'll put it right.
