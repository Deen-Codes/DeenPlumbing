Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This policy explains how Deen Plumbing ("we", "I") collects and uses your personal information. We keep things simple and only collect what we need to do the job.

What we collect

When you contact us by phone, email, or the request-a-job form, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, postcode/address, and details of the work you need.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and arrange or carry out work.
  • To provide quotes, invoices, and any certificates (e.g. gas safety records).
  • To keep records we're legally required to hold.

Sharing

We don't sell your data. We only share information where necessary to complete work or where required by law (for example, gas safety records).

How long we keep it

We keep job and safety records for as long as required by law and good practice, then delete them securely.

Your rights

You can ask to see the information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it where we're not legally required to keep it. To make a request, just get in touch.
